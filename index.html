Who are We

We are Spiritual Soul Sistaz with Purified hands. We are a organization of men and women who volunteer to get into the heart of our communities to bring prayer, supplies , clothing and food to the less forunate. We scour the community in cars and on foot; finding those who have shunned away from society to bring them hope. We assist all people, Men, Women and children no one is turned away.

How do we operate

We accept all donations; clothing, food and monetary. We are currently looking to partner with shelters in the Detroit, MI and Toledo, OH areas.
